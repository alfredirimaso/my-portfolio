About

Irimaso Alfred is a Ugandan computer scientist, educator, and technology enthusiast affiliated with Kabale University. He is active in teaching, mentoring, and advancing digital learning in higher education. He integrates modern tools and open learning platforms to improve student outcomes.

Teaching

  • Computer Applications

    Practical computer skills and productivity tools for students in multiple disciplines.

  • Database Design

    Relational modelling, normalization, SQL, and practical design for real systems.

  • Software Engineering Fundamentals

    Software lifecycle, requirements, design, testing and teamwork practices.

  • Networking Concepts

    Basics of networking, protocols, and configuration for small networks.

Community & Leadership

Alfred contributes to university ICT initiatives and the ISACA Kabale University Student Chapter, promoting cybersecurity awareness and professional growth among students.
